<h2 id="timeline">Timeline of Events</h2>
<div class="spaced">
<ul>
<li><img src="/img/alert.png" width="16" height="16"> <span class="style1"><a href="https://www.hhs.gov/about/news/2020/01/31/secretary-azar-declares-public-health-emergency-us-2019-novel-coronavirus.html">On January 31, HHS declared Coronavirus a Public Health Emergency in the US</a></span><br>
As of Jan. 31, the Wuhan coronavirus is officially a public health emergency in the United States, Alex Azar, secretary of the US Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announced at a White House press briefing.</li>
<li><img src="/img/alert.png" width="16" height="16"> On Jan. 31, the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) <strong>issued a federal quarantine for 14 days</strong> affecting the 195 American evacuees from Wuhan, China. Starting Sunday, Feb. 2, U.S. citizens, permanent residents and immediate family who have visited China's Hubei province will undergo a <strong>mandatory 14 days quarantine</strong> and, if they have visited other parts of China, they would be screened at airports and asked to self-quarantine for 14 days. The last time the CDC had issued a quarantine was over 50 years ago in the 1960s, for smallpox.</li>
<li><img src="/img/alert.png" width="16" height="16"> President Donald Trump signed an order on Jan. 31 for the U.S. to<strong> deny entry to foreign nationals who traveled to China</strong> within the preceding two weeks, aside from the immediate family of U.S. citizens.</li>
<li>On Jan. 30, the CDC had confirmed the<strong> first case of person to person transmission in the U.S.: </strong><strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-12">12</a>]</sup></strong> the husband of the Chicago, Illinois case who had returned from Wuhan, China on Jan. 13 and who tested positive for the virus on Jan. 24).</li>
<li>CDC stated on Jan. 30 that &quot;It is likely there will be more cases of 2019-nCoV reported in the U.S. in the coming days and weeks, including more person-to-person spread.&quot;<strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-12">12</a>]</sup></strong></li>
<li>The virus had been confirmed in <strong>5 states</strong>.</li>
<li>On Jan. 31, <strong> New York City</strong> health officials vehemently denied the rumor regarding a coronavirus case in the city .<strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-13">13</a>]</sup></strong>. On Feb. 1, however, the city's health commissioner did report that there was a test being performed on a person under 40 who had returned from China, developed matching symptoms, and tested negative to the seasonal flu.</li>
<li><strong>Most US patients had recently visited Wuhan</strong>.</li>
<li>All of the first five U.S. cases were described as <strong>mild</strong>.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.cnn.com/2020/01/31/health/washington-coronavirus-study-nejm/index.html?utm_term=link&utm_medium=social&utm_content=2020-02-01T00">A study on the first US case of novel coronavirus detailed mild symptoms followed by pneumonia</a></li>
</ul>
</div>
<h3>U.S. Airlines suspended ALL flights between the U.S. and China </h3>
<p>On Friday, January 31, Delta, American and United announced they would temporarily suspend all of their mainland China flights in response to the coronavirus outbreak.<strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-14">14</a>]</sup></strong></p>
<p> Prior to this January 31 announcement: </p>
<ul>
<li><strong>UNITED AIRLINES</strong><br>
on Jan. 28 had announced it would cut 24 flights between the U.S. and China for the first week of February.</li>
<li><strong>AMERICAN AIRLINES</strong><br>
on Jan. 29 had announced it would suspend flights from Los Angeles to Shanghai and Beijing from Feb. 9 through March 27, 2020. It will maintain its flight schedules (10 daily A/R) from Dallas-Fort Worth to Shanghai and Beijing, as well as from Los Angeles and Dallas-Fort Worth to Hong Kong.</li>
<li><strong>DELTA<br>
</strong> had not adjusted its schedule of direct flights from the U.S. to China. It is the only airline with direct flights to not take action so far. </li>
</ul>
The White House was considering issuing a ban on flights between the United States and China, as of late Jan. 28<strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-11">11</a>]</sup></strong>.
Italy has announced on January 31 that it was suspending all flights to and from China following the first 2 cases of coronavirus in Italy.
<h3>Travel Alert: Do Not Travel to China</h3>
<ul>
<li>The U.S. State Department on January 30 issued a <strong><a href="https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/traveladvisories/traveladvisories/china-travel-advisory.html">Level 4: Do Not Travel to China Alert</a></strong> <strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-4">4</a>]</sup></strong> (the highest level of alert). </li>
<li>Previously, on January 29, the advisory was set at a lower &quot;Level 3: Reconsider Travel&quot; advising not to travel to Hubei Province: (Level 4) and reconsider travel to the remainder of China (Level 3).</li>
<li>The CDC on Jan. 28 issued a <a href="https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/notices/warning/novel-coronavirus-china">Level 3 Warning</a>, recommending that travelers <strong>avoid all nonessential travel to China </strong><sup></sup><strong><sup>[<a href="#ref-5">5</a>]</sup></strong>.<br>
</li>
</ul>

    function validateEmail(email) {
        var re = /^(([^<>()[\]\\.,;:\s@\"]+(\.[^<>()[\]\\.,;:\s@\"]+)*)|(\".+\"))@((\[[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}\])|(([a-zA-Z\-0-9]+\.)+[a-zA-Z]{2,}))$/;
        return re.test(email);
    }

    function scrollIntoViewIfNeeded(target) {
        var rect = target.getBoundingClientRect();
        if (rect.bottom > window.innerHeight) {
            target.scrollIntoView(false);
        }
        if (rect.top < 0) {
            target.scrollIntoView();
        }
    }

    function showErrorMessage(msg) {
        scrollIntoViewIfNeeded($(".statusMsg")[0]);
        $('.statusMsg').html('<span style="color:red;">' + msg + '</span>').fadeOut(200).fadeIn(200);
    }

    function showConfirmForm(e) {

        var form = $('#contactForm');

        var all_empty = true;

        if (form.find('#states_select option:selected').text() != '') {


            all_empty = false;
            $('#confirm_state').html('<strong>USA state/territory: </strong>' + form.find('#states_select option:selected').text());
        } else {

            showErrorMessage('"State" field is required');
            return false;
            $('#confirm_state').html('');
        }

        Object.keys(reports_types).forEach(function(report_type) {

            var report_type_label = reports_types[report_type];

            if (form.find('#new_' + report_type).val() != '') {
                all_empty = false;
                $('#confirm_new_' + report_type).html('<strong>New ' + report_type_label +
                    ': </strong>' + form.find('#new_' + report_type).val());
            } else {
                $('#confirm_new_' + report_type).html('');
            }

            if (form.find('#total_' + report_type).val() != '') {
                all_empty = false;
                $('#confirm_total_' + report_type).html('<strong>Total ' + report_type_label +
                    ': </strong>' + form.find('#total_' + report_type).val());
            } else {
                $('#confirm_total_' + report_type).html('');
            }

        });

        if (form.find('#counties_select option:selected').text() != '') {

            all_empty = false;
            $('#confirm_county').html('<strong>USA county: </strong>' + form.find('#counties_select option:selected').text());

        } else {


            $('#confirm_county').html('');
        }
        if (form.find('#email').val() != '') {

            if (!validateEmail(form.find('#email').val())) {

                showErrorMessage('Bad email address format, please fix');
                return false;

            }

            all_empty = false;
            $('#confirm_email').html('<strong>Email: </strong>' + form.find('#email').val());
        } else {
            $('#confirm_email').html('');
        }

        if (form.find('#url').val() != '') {
            all_empty = false;

            $('#confirm_url').html('<strong>URL: </strong>' + form.find('#url').val());

            $('#confirm_url').html('');

        if (form.find('#message').val() != '') {
            all_empty = false;
            $('#confirm_message').html('<strong>Comment: </strong>' + form.find('#message').val());
        } else {
            $('#confirm_message').html('');
        }


        e.preventDefault();
        $('#contactModalForm').modal('hide');
        $('.statusMsg').html('');
        $('#contactModalFormConfirm').modal('show');


    }
